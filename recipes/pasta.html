Pasta

Description

This is a delicious pasta recipe that you can make in under 30 minutes!

Whether you're cooking for family or friends, this recipe is easy to follow and always turns out great. Follow the steps below to recreate this wonderful dish at home!

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine or tagliatelle pasta
  • Mushrooms (cremini, button, or oyster mushrooms)
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream (or cooking cream)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the mushrooms and sauté until they are tender and browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Stir well to combine with the mushrooms.
  4. Gradually add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a little bit of pasta water to thin it out.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss it in the sauce until well coated.
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy your delicious pasta!
