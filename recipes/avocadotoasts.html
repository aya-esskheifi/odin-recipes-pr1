Avocado Toasts

Description

A simple and healthy breakfast or brunch option that's perfect for a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, cherry tomatoes, or poached eggs

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough bread slices until they are golden and crispy.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook the eggs according to your preference (fried, scrambled, or poached).
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice of toasted bread.
  5. Top each slice with a cooked egg.
  6. Optionally, sprinkle red pepper flakes for some heat or add cherry tomatoes for extra flavor and color.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious avocado toasts!
