Morrocan tea

Description

A traditional Moroccan tea that is both refreshing and aromatic, perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 spoons of green tea leaves
  • 1 spoon of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups of boiling water

Instructions

  1. Place the green tea leaves in a teapot.
  2. Pour the boiling water over the tea leaves and let it steep for about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea into cups, removing the tea leaves.
  4. Add sugar to taste and stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
  5. Serve the Moroccan tea hot and enjoy its refreshing flavor!
